On the third day we arrived in the Haksha Market area to enjoy some local foods, And you can't help but visit Museum Island and Lustgarten Park. We took a direct flight and landed at Schiphol Airport. Third time for me a city that has long been a familiar place, We started the fifth day with a simply amazing breakfast. On the eighth day we had breakfast, And we said goodbye to Berlin and Amsterdam.
