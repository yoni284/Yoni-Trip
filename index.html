MY Trip Plan

We originally planned this trip in February 2020.
If that date sounds familiar, it's no coincidence –
that's when the world was just starting to grapple with the coronavirus pandemic.
It completely derailed our plans. But we didn't give up hope.
Our WhatsApp group stayed active, and we eventually rescheduled the trip for July 2023.

Berlin

We began our trip to Berlin in the afternoon at the iconic Alexanderplatz square. From there, we visited a local festival to immerse ourselves in the local culture before heading to the historic Berlin Wall. On the second day, we toured Berlin's well-known sites, followed by a visit to the entrance of the largest zoo in Europe. On the third day, we explored the Hackescher Markt area to enjoy some local cuisine, and of course, we couldn't miss Museum Island and Lustgarten Park. In the evening, we had a spectacular time at the local clubs. On the fourth and final day in Berlin, we visited the prestigious Kurfürstendamm area. You could spend an entire day there and still want more.

Amsterdam

We took a direct flight and landed at Schiphol Airport. For me, it was my third visit to this familiar city. Our fifth day began with a delicious breakfast, followed by exploring the Leidseplein area. We spent the entire day immersing ourselves in the neighborhood. On day six, we embarked on a walking tour of the city, visiting attractions like Madame Tussauds and the Heineken factory. We concluded the day with a delightful dinner. On day seven, we rented a car and took a day trip to the enchanting Efteling amusement park. The evening was spent unwinding at a cocktail bar. After a leisurely breakfast on day eight, we bid farewell to both Berlin and Amsterdam.
